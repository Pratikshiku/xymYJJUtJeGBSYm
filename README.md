## Graduate Information Recruitment Platform

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/38ef863296364ac4814b1d2202ca58aa/ghb20250917) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can be used to run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): graduates, enterprises, administrators.

**Graduate Functions:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Personal Center
4. Personal Resume
5. Enterprise Online Presentations (favorite, browse, comment)
6. Job Application, Job Consultation, Favorite Jobs
7. Add Job Search Information
8. Forum Information (posting)
9. Online Exams
10. Recruitment News
11. My Favorites
12. My Posts
13. My Exam Records
14. Wrong Answer Book
15. Interview Replies


**Enterprise Functions:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Personal Center (change password, personal information)
4. Online Presentations
5. Job Postings
6. Information Consultation
7. Job Applications
8. Online Interviews
9. Interview Replies
10. Exam Paper Management
11. Question Management
12. Exam Management

**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center
3. Enterprise Management
4. Online Presentation Management
5. Job Posting Management
6. Graduate Management
7. Personal Resume Management
8. Job Search Information Management
9. Information Consultation Management
10. Job Application Management
11. Online Interview Management
12. Interview Reply Management
13. Exam Paper Management
14. Question Management
15. Administrator Management
16. Forum Management
17. System Management - Recruitment News
18. System Management - Carousel Management
19. Exam Management
